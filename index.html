About Me

Hello! I'm Vasundhara Shaw, a postdoctoral researcher in astrophysics at the University of Manchester.

My work explores cosmic ray propagation, the Galactic magnetic field, and polarised synchrotron emission, using surveys like C-BASS and S-PASS. I also simulate ultra-high energy cosmic ray trajectories with CRPropa3.

Beyond research, I enjoy mentoring, outreach, and supporting equity in science. I’m actively involved in collaborations like POSSUM, and I'm currently working on multiple first-author publications.
