About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in astrophysics at the University of Manchester, with a focus on cosmic ray propagation, Galactic magnetic field (GMF) modeling, and polarised radio sky observations.

I lead comparisons of GMF models using full-sky 5 GHz synchrotron data from C-BASS and S-PASS, and continue work from my PhD on ultra-high energy cosmic ray simulations using CRPropa3. My research integrates theoretical models with high-performance computing and advanced sky analysis tools.
