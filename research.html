Research Interests

  • Galactic magnetic field modeling and template fitting
  • Ultra-high energy cosmic ray (UHECR) diffusion and deflections
  • Analysis of polarised synchrotron maps from C-BASS and S-PASS
  • Numerical simulations with CRPropa3
  • High-performance computing and big data astrophysics

Technical Tools

  • CRPropa3, Healpy, Astropy, NiFTy
  • Python, C++, Fortran
  • Cluster computing, MPI, HPC environments
  • Scientific visualisation with Matplotlib and Plotly
